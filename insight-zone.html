📊 Insight Zone – Data That Empowers You

Mission: Give users the power to track their activity, reflect on patterns, and make smarter decisions across career, emotional wellness, and productivity — all in 8 languages.

1. Personal Usage Dashboard

Visual time logs for Career Zone, Emotional Journal, Productivity Tools, Language Learning — by day, week, or month.

2. Progress Tracker (Streaks & Wins)

Earn badges like “3 Days of Healing” or “5 Gigs Completed.” Stay motivated.

3. Smart Notifications

Get personalized nudges based on your activity: “Great job prioritizing your wellbeing!”

4. Offline Data Sync

Track progress without internet — syncs when online returns.

5. Multilingual Reports

Weekly insights in Hausa, Igbo, Yoruba, Pidgin, Swahili, French, Arabic, Zulu. Download or share PDF.

6. Custom Goals & Reminders

“Apply to 5 jobs this week” – Set your own reminders and stay on track.

7. AI Insights & Suggestions

Get personalized feedback: “Want to update your CV?”

8. Mood Analytics (Emotional Zone Sync)

Chart your emotional patterns. See growth over time.

9. Data Privacy First

All tracking is optional. You control what’s collected and can delete anytime.

Languages Supported: Hausa, Igbo, Yoruba, Pidgin, Swahili, French, Arabic, Zulu.
